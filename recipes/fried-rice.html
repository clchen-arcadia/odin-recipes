Fried Rice

Description

A stable of Asian cuisine, for every culture with an association with rice, there is a recipe for fried rice. There are many, many versions of this dish and a lot of room for experimentation and personal touches and tweaks.

The key to this recipe is using day old rice from the fridge. This is because rice that has been dried out with separate from each other and fry well in the oil rather than stick to each other in clumps.

Ingredients

  • Day old cooked rice
  • Egg
  • Vegetable or canola oil
  • Bacon or spam or meat of your choice
  • Green onion
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Crack and mix 2 eggs
  2. Cut your meat into small dice like pieces
  3. Thaw your peas if frozen
  4. Cook your meat on low heat to render the fat and cook it golden brown
  5. Put the cooked meat to one side and add some oil and heat until smoking
  6. Fry the eggs until fragrant and season
  7. Add back in the cooked meat and peas and toss together on medium heat
  8. Add the rice and add some more oil on top and stir fry and season with some salt
  9. Add soy sauce from the edge of the wok to color the rice
  10. Finally, throw in the sliced green onion, give a final stir, and serve
  11. Enjoy!
